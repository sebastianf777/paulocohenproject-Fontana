Contact/About

I am a central London based freelance photographer, specialising in travel and people photography. Working on commissions in the UK and abroad shooting images for magazines, travel guides and business publications. Clients include AB InBev, Berlitz, Sunday Times Travel, Penguin Random House, Twilio, Korean Air, UCL, Imperial College, Imagination Technologies and National Geographic Traveller. Currently finalist Portrait of Humanity 2021, Portrait for Britain 2019, cover of British Journal of Photography and finalist in The Budapest Diary exhibition 2019
